NMAP

##Nmap sends SYN packet from the TCP 3-way handshake and waits for SYN-ACK reply but never completes handshake afterwards. used for network reconnaissance, vulnerability assessment, and security auditing. Nmap –6 ##tells to use IPv6

port types:

  • Open ports:port where service is actively listening and can be targeted
  • Filtered ports:Nmap could not determine if they are open or closed. may be behind firewall or have security measures.
  • Closed ports: ports where no service is listening, and they are not a potential attack vector.

NMAP Engine

Nmap Scripting engine(NSE) (official Link) Scripts are written in LUA programming language.

  • Structure run all scripts in category: nmap [Scan Options] --script=
  • Example of use: nmap -p 80 --script=http-title example.com
  • Example of 2 categories: nmap -p 80 --script=smb-enum-users,smb-enum-shares example.com

HYDRA

Hydra is used for password hacking mainly for brute force attacks

  • fast online password cracking tool,
  • can perform rapid dictionary attacks against more than 50 Protocols, including Telnet, RDP, SSH, FTP, HTTP, HTTPS, SMB.
  • USAGE: hydra -t 4 -l dale -P /usr/share/wordlists/rockyou.txt -vV 10.10.10.6 ftp
