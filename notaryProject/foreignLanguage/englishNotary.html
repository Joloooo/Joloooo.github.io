Power of Attorney for a Declared Vehicle

Documents Required for the Contract:

  1. The grantor's ID card, driver's license, or passport (must not be expired, and the person in the photo must resemble the grantor)
  2. Vehicle's technical passport (must not be expired)
  3. The presence of the grantee is not required

Contract Value: 25 GEL

Power of Attorney for an Undeclared Vehicle

Documents Required for the Contract:

  1. The grantor's ID card, driver's license, or passport (must not be expired, and the person in the photo must resemble the grantor)
  2. Contract or other document confirming that the vehicle belongs to the grantor
  3. The presence of the grantee is not required

Contract Value: 25 GEL

Real Estate Power of Attorney

Documents Required for the Contract:

  1. The grantor's ID card, driver's license, or passport (must not be expired, and the person in the photo must resemble the grantor)
  2. Real estate cadastral code, where the grantor is listed as the owner
  3. The presence of the grantee is not required

Contract Value: 25 GEL

Power of Attorney for a Lawyer

Documents Required for the Contract:

  1. The grantor's ID card, driver's license, or passport (must not be expired, and the person in the photo must resemble the grantor)
  2. The grantor must know the lawyer's details: personal ID number, first name, last name
  3. The lawyer's presence is not required

Contract Value: 25 GEL

Power of Attorney for the Bank

Documents Required for the Contract:

  1. The grantor's ID card, driver's license, or passport (must not be expired, and the person in the photo must resemble the grantor)
  2. Bank details
  3. The grantor must fully understand each right they are granting to the bank. If the person does not understand the rights being granted and is requesting the power of attorney using a bank template without understanding, the power of attorney will not be issued.

Contract Value: 25 GEL

Online Power of Attorney for a Declared Vehicle

Documents Required for the Contract:

  1. The grantor's ID card, driver's license, or passport (must not be expired, and the person in the photo must resemble the grantor)
  2. Vehicle's technical passport (must not be expired)
  3. Two witnesses must come to the notary office, who
    1. Personally know the grantor
    2. At least one of the witnesses must not be a relative of the grantor
    3. Both witnesses must have a valid ID card, driver's license, or passport (must not be expired, and the person in the photo must resemble the witness)
  4. The presence of the grantee is not required

Contract Value: 65 GEL
